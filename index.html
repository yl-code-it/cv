    Yu Lin, Ph.D, P.Eng

    With over 7 years of R&D experience in sensing technology, Yu Lin has a track record of developing industrial robotic vision systems that automatically inspect product quality and measure process variation. As a Technology Development Senior Specialist, Yu is responsible for developing the sensing systems that automate the inspection in automotive manufacturing. On a personal front, Yu lives in Ontario, Canada with his wife and is a pround father of 3 children. Yu's favorite way to unwind when not in front of computer is to explore the nature and outdoors with kids. He holds a PhD degree in Aerospace Eng., and MASc degree in Mechanical Eng. from Ryerson Univeristy.

    Interested area: mechatronics, automation, sensors, robotics, machine vision, automated inspection, artificial intelligence, etc.
